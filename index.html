About Us

GlassEDGE is a free, student-run platform designed to help DECA competitors prepare smarter and win more. Created by passionate competitors, it provides AI-powered roleplays, written event tools, and personalized feedback to make practice efficient and realistic. With a focus on usability, performance, and authenticity, GlassEDGE empowers students and schools to reach their peak potential — no paywalls, no logins, just preparation built for you.
